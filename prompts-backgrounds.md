# Background Prompts — Hyperreality Key (Levels 2–7 + Victory)
## Premium Prompt Engineering — Best Practices

**Global settings for ALL prompts:**
- Aspect ratio: 1920×1080 landscape, 16:9
- Style: Cinematic cyberpunk / neon-noir digital art. Think Blade Runner 2049 meets Control (Remedy game). High contrast, volumetric lighting, deep blacks.
- Output: Photorealistic 3D render quality, Unreal Engine 5 aesthetic. PNG, no text unless specified.
- Usage: Background layer rendered at 15% opacity behind a dark gradient overlay (#0a0a0f → transparent), so designs should be VISIBLE at low opacity — avoid tiny details, favor bold silhouettes, strong light sources, and clear atmospheric shapes.
- Color grading: Deep shadows (#0a0a0f), lifted blacks, subtle bloom on neon sources.

```
- Smoke/haze in the air catching the amber light

ATMOSPHERE: The room feels frantic, emotionally charged. The amber light is overwhelming — like the room is on fire with misinformation. The red siren light creates a sense of danger and urgency.

NO visible text except "FEAR", "OUTRAGE", "CLICKS" on papers and "FAKE" stamp on screen.
```

**Accent:** #8b5cf6 (Purple), #c4b5fd (Light Lavender)

**Prompt:**
```
Cinematic 3D render, Unreal Engine 5 quality. A surreal dreamscape where reality layers are fractured, 16:9 landscape 1920×1080.

COMPOSITION: A desert landscape at purple twilight. In the foreground-right, a massive TikTok screen on a billboard frame shows a doctor's face — but it's glitching. Half the face is photorealistic, the other half is digital circuit patterns with purple code streaming down. The face is recognizable as human but deeply wrong — uncanny valley.

CENTER: A Wild West saloon facade standing alone in the desert — but it's OBVIOUSLY fake. It's a painted plywood prop with visible wooden support beams behind it. Up close you can see the painted windows don't have glass. It's a simulacrum — a copy of something that never really existed in this context.

BACKGROUND LAYERS (stacked at different depths):
- Layer 1 (closest): A transparent cracked glass panel
- Layer 2 (middle): Same scene but slightly different — saloon is shifted, TikTok face has different expression
- Layer 3 (farthest): Same scene again but the saloon is now a real building, not a prop
- These layers create a sense of reality slipping — you can't tell which layer is "real"

LIGHTING & COLOR:
- Primary: lavender (#a78bfa) fog and ambient light
- Accent: deep purple (#8b5cf6) shadows, light lavender (#c4b5fd) highlights
- The desert sand has a slight purple tint
- The TikTok screen glows with an unnatural purple-white light
- Volumetric fog with purple God rays

DETAILS:
- A cracked mirror on the ground reflecting something that shouldn't be there — a blue sky in a purple-lit scene

- The doctor's face on screen has one human eye and one digital/glitch eye

- The horizon line is slightly tilted, adding to the disorientation

ATMOSPHERE: Deeply uncanny, surreal, intellectually unsettling. This is Baudrillard's hyperreality — the simulation has replaced the real so effectively that reality itself becomes questionable. The fake facade in a fake desert on a fake screen — layers of simulacra.
```
