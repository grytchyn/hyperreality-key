# Background Generation Prompts — Hyperreality Key

## Global Style Frame (applies to ALL backgrounds)

**Format:** 1920×1080px, PNG, landscape
**Composition:** Horizontal triptych — left 25% and right 25% have dense illustrations, center 50% is intentionally open/sparse for UI overlay
**Art Direction:** Cyberpunk-meets-academic aesthetic. Think: Blade Runner 2049 ambient lighting + Papers, Please dystopian bureaucracy + digital glitch textures
**Palette:** Each background uses ONE dominant accent color (per-level) + black/dark charcoal `#0a0a0f` base + subtle warm rim tones
**Texture:** Subtle scanline overlay (CRT monitor effect at 10% opacity), vignette darkening at edges
**Lighting:** Ambient glow from the side elements bleeding into the center. Key light source positioned at the edges casting inward
**Opacity treatment:** These are background images — they sit at 15% opacity behind a dark gradient overlay in-game. Design them as if looking through frosted glass at a brightly lit scene. High contrast, vivid colors, so they remain visible and atmospheric through the dark overlay.
**NO text or UI elements** in the image itself
**NO central focal point** — the center must remain visually quiet
