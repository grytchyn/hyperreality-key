# Universal Game Background — 1 Prompt for All 12 Levels

## Format

**1920×1080px**, PNG, landscape, transparent if possible or black base.

**Composition:** 3 gleich breite vertikale Panels — links 33% illustriert, Mitte 33% leer, rechts 33% illustriert.

**Art Direction:** Cyberpunk-meets-academic. Blade Runner 2049 ambient lighting meets dystopian bureaucracy. Dark and moody, high contrast. Matte painting quality.

**Palette:** The image should be predominantly dark/neutral (`#0a0a0f` base) with subtle cool-toned highlights (deep blue, muted teal, faint purple). Keep it **neutral enough to work with any accent color** — the UI will add red, green, orange, pink overlays per-level.

**Textur:** Subtiler CRT scanline overlay at 10% opacity. Vignette at edges. Granular film grain at 3%.

**Center 33% bleibt absichtlich leer** — kein Möbel, keine Figur, nur leerer Boden + dunkle Atmosphäre + leichter Fog. Das UI der App wird hier platziert.

**NO text, NO logos, NO UI elements im Bild.**

**Opacity im Spiel:** 15% mit dark overlay — also hoher Kontrast, damit es durchscheint.

## Prompt

```
Epic 1920×1080 landscape background, cyberpunk dystopian command center, black (#0a0a0f) base with dark blue-teal accent tones. Triptych composition: three equal vertical panels each 33% wide.

Left 33% — A towering wall of CRT monitors and oscilloscopes, some screens showing static, others displaying cascading columns of code (Matrix-style). Tangled cables hang down. A few old-fashioned filing cabinets beneath. Dim blue-teal ambient light.

Center 33% — Completely empty. Dark concrete or metal floor receding into deep shadow. Slight volumetric fog at floor level. No furniture, no objects, no people. The void is intentional — UI will go here.

Right 33% — Heavy industrial server racks with blinking LEDs, steam pipes, a large analog clock with broken face. Faint purple-teal glow from the electronics. A single worn leather office chair facing away.

Subtle CRT scanline overlay at 10%. Dark vignette at all edges. Film grain at 3%. High contrast so it remains visible behind dark UI overlay. Matte painting apocalyptic nihilistic atmosphere. NO text, NO central objects, NO people.
```

# Victory Screen Background — 1 Prompt

## Format

**1920×1080px**, PNG, landscape, black base.

**Gleiche 33/33/33 Composition:** links 33% illustriert, Mitte 33% leer, rechts 33% illustriert.

**Farbe:** Warm gold-orange `#fbbf24` Akzente + deep purple `#8b5cf6` Highlights — feierlich, triumphierend, aber nicht hell. Eher "die Simulation wurde besiegt, die Dämmerung bricht an" Atmosphäre.

**Center 33% bleibt leer** für Victory UI (Score, Stats, Buttons).

## Prompt

```
Epic 1920×1080 landscape background, victory after escaping the simulation. Black base with gold (#fbbf24) and deep purple (#8b5cf6) accents. Triptych composition: three equal vertical panels.

Left 33% — Shattered remains of deception: broken CRT televisions with cracked screens, fragments of social media icons (thumbs-up, bird silhouette, camera) floating in zero gravity, each fracture glowing warm gold. Debris suspended mid-explosion. Dark purple backdrop.

Center 33% — Completely empty. A polished dark marble floor receding into distance. A single faint golden keyhole symbol embedded in the floor at center, warm gold light beam rising from it upward into darkness. No furniture, no objects, no people. Just the light shaft and atmospheric haze.

Right 33% — Floating orbs in orbital paths: a philosophy book, a brain with neural spark, scales of justice, an open eye, a shield, a key. Each orb connected by thin lines of purple light. Like a holographic diagram of tools mastered.

Subtle golden particles floating in the air. CRT scanline at 5% (lighter — victory is clearer). Warm ambient light, grand and solemn. High contrast. NO text, NO central objects beyond the keyhole on the floor. The simulation has been defeated — truth has a throne now.
```
